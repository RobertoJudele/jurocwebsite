Who We Are

Juroc Solutions SRL

Founded in Romania, Juroc Solutions SRL is the company behind a sports coaching discovery platform designed to bridge the gap between athletes and professional coaches. Whether you're looking to get fit, sharpen your football skills or master a new sport, our platform makes finding the right coach effortless.

We believe everyone deserves access to quality coaching. Our platform connects athletes with certified coaches across Romania and beyond, offering a seamless way to discover, book and train with professionals in your chosen sport.

  • Legally registered entity in Romania (SRL)
  • Verified coach profiles with certifications
  • GDPR-compliant data practices
  • Secure booking and in-app communication
  • Available in multiple sport disciplines
  • Dedicated support team
