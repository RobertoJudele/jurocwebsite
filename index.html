Who We Are

Juroc Solutions SRL

Founded in Romania, Juroc Solutions SRL is a registered limited liability company (Societate cu Răspundere Limitată) specialising in the design, development and maintenance of software products for businesses of all sizes.

Our team of experienced engineers, designers and project managers is passionate about turning complex problems into elegant, high-performance digital products. We work with start-ups, scale-ups and established enterprises across Europe and beyond.

  • Legally registered entity in Romania (SRL)
  • Transparent contracts and clear pricing
  • GDPR-compliant development practices
  • Dedicated project manager for every client
  • Full IP transfer upon project completion
  • Post-launch maintenance & support packages
